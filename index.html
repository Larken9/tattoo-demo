FAQ

How do I prepare for my first tattoo?

Arrive well-rested, hydrated, and with a light meal in you. Avoid alcohol 24 hours before.

What’s your cancellation policy?

We require 48 hours notice to change or cancel your appointment or your deposit is forfeit.

What should I bring to my session?

Bring a photo ID, any reference images, and wear clothing that gives easy access to the area you’re tattooing.
